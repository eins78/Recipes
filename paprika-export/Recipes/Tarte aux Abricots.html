Tarte aux Abricots

★★★★★

_Proven, Dessert, French

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 35 minutes Servings: Portions 8 people Source: menu-vegetarien.com

Ingredients

1 shortcrust pastry, vegan

500 g apricots, well ripe

25 g wheat flour

1 cás maizena

1 tbsp agave syrup

1/2 tbsp vanilla extract

25 cl vegetable milk (almond milk for example)

Directions

PREPARATION OF THE RECIPE

Preheat the oven to 180°C. Bake the shortcrust pastry for 5 to 6 minutes.

Remove the kernels from the apricots. Blanch them for 3 minutes in a pot of boiling water. Egoutter.

Put the maizena, flour, agave syrup and vanilla extract in a saucepan. Warm up for a few seconds by mixing any stop. Pour the vegetable milk. Let cook over low heat, whisking until the cream thickens.

Spread the cream over the dough. Garnish with the apricots, part cut upwards and cook at 180 °C 25 to 30 minutes.
