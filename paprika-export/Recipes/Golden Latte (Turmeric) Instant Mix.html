Golden Latte (Turmeric) Instant Mix

_Proven

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 5 min Servings: 9 Source: pickuplimes.com

Ingredients

To make the powdered mix:

1 Tbsp (9 g) ground turmeric

2 tsp (5 g) ground cinnamon

2 tsp (8 g) vanilla sugar

1½ tsp (7 g) granulated sugar

½ tsp (1 g) ground cardamom

¼ + ⅛ tsp (0.7 g) ground ginger*

1 pinch (0.1 g) ground nutmeg

1 pinch (0.1 g) ground black pepper


To make the lattes:

9 cups (2160 mL) unsweetened oat milk (1 cup milk per serving)**

Directions

To make the latte mix, mix all the ingredients together in a bowl or jar until well combined.

When you're ready to make a latte, add 1 tsp of the mix to a cup, followed by 1 cup (240 mL) of hot oat milk (or any other milk of your liking). Top with some foamed milk and cinnamon, and enjoy!

Storage

The mix can be stored in an airtight container or mason jar in a cool and dry place for up to 6 months.

Notes

* Add more ginger if you like a kick.

** Nutrition tip: aim to use plant-based milk that is fortified with calcium.
