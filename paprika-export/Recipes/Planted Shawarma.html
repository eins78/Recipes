Planted Shawarma

Ingredients

Marinade:

1 soy cream

1/2 lemon juiced

1/4 cup vinegar

1 tbsp olive oil

2 cloves garlic (crushed)

1/2 tsp salt

1 tsp pepper

2 cardamom pods

1/2 tsp ground allspice

1 tsp paprika

1 tsp cumin

1/2 tsp coriander powder

2 tsp corn starch


Shawarma:

1 pkg planted chicken

1 small onion, squared

Directions

Combine and let sit in the fridge overnight or at least 2 hours.

Preheat oven 200C.
Add quatered onion.

Bake for 15, until the liquid evaporated from the marinade and it gets firm.
stir with a spatula.
grill for 5min/until crisp and golden brown on top.
