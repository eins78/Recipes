Cabbage Salad (Middle Eastern Style)

★★★★★

_Proven, Condiments, Middle Eastern, Side Dishes

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Source: cheftariq.com

Ingredients

½ Head of Cabbage large

¼ cup Lemon Juice

⅓ cup Olive Oil

3 Garlic Cloves

¼ tsp Salt

1 tbsp Nigella, Black Sesam or Poppy Seeds

Directions

To a blender add the lemon juice, olive oil, garlic and salt. Blend until smooth.

Shred or finely chop cabbage.

Add all ingredients to a bowl and mix well.

Finish by adding the poppy seeds and mixing well.
