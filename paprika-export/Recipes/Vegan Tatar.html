Vegan Tatar

Servings: Serves 4-6 Source: instagram.com

Ingredients

100 g rice cakes

500 ml vegetable broth, warm

25 g soy or pea mince, dry

60 g tomato paste

40 g mustard

160 g ketchup

40 ml beetroot juice

40 g capers

80 g pickles

80 g onions

8 g jalapeños, optional

1 tsp salt

Directions

Crumble rice cakes and add 400 ml warm vegetable broth. Soak chopped soy in remaining broth. After about 5 minutes, drain and add to rice cake mixture.

Add ketchup, tomato paste, mustard and beet juice to the mixture and let stand for about 10 minutes.

Meanwhile, finely chop pickles, onions, capers and jalapeños. Add to the mixture and season with salt, pepper and Tabasco.

Garnish the tartare with onions, caper apples, jalapeños or onion sprouts. Serve with toast and vegan butter or margarine.
