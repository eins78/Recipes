Dumpling Soup

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 20 min Servings: 4 Source: drveganblog.com

Directions

Why You’ll Love This Easy Dumpling Soup

Quick and Simple: Ready in just 30 minutes, it’s a perfect option for weeknight dinners.

Versatile Ingredients: Use your favorite type of noodles and dumplings for a customizable meal.

Rich Flavor: The curry paste, garlic, ginger, and tahini blend to create a bold and creamy broth that’s unforgettable.

Prepare a rich flavor base by combining curry paste, garlic, ginger, tahini, and soy sauce, then pouring sizzling hot oil over the mixture to unlock its bold aromas.

Cook the noodles, blanch the bok choy, and boil the dumplings while reserving the cooking water to create a smooth, aromatic broth.

Assemble by layering noodles, bok choy, and dumplings in bowls, then pour the hot broth over and garnish with scallions, sesame seeds, and chili oil.

With just 30 minutes of prep and cook time, you’ll have a vibrant, restaurant-quality soup ready to enjoy.

Pro Tips for Making the Best Dumpling Soup

Choose the Right Noodles: This recipe works with a variety of noodles – ramen, udon, soba, or even glass noodles. Each adds its unique texture to the soup.

Dumpling Options: Frozen dumplings are a time-saver, but homemade dumplings can make the dish feel extra special.

Customize the Spice: Prefer milder flavors? Use a mild curry paste or reduce the chili oil. For spice lovers, add more chili flakes or opt for a spicy curry paste.
