Vegan Egg (Moong Dal Omelette)

Servings: Servings: 2 huge Source: copymethat.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup moong dal

3/4 tsp kala namak

1/2 tsp onion powder

1/4 tsp ground turmeric

2 tsp nutritional yeast

1/2 cup + 2 Tbs unsweetened plain plant milk

1 Tbsp olive oil

2 Tbsp chickpea flour

3/4 tsp baking powder

Oil for cooking

Directions

In a bowl, rinse the moong Dal water about 4-5 times. After the last rinse, cover with 1 cup water and let soak for at least 3 hours and up to 8.

Strain the moong Dal and add to a blender with the rest of the ingredients. Process until smooth. Taste for salt. (You can pour this in to a container and keep in the fridge for up to a week.)

Oil a pan and set over medium low heat. Pour 1/4 to 1/2 the mix in the pan and spread out into an even circle. Cover and cook until edges are brown (about two minutes) then flip. Cover and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Add cheese or filling if desired. Fold over and serve immediately.

Notes

Shown served with my vegan “Taylor ham” with non dairy cheddar on a bagel.
