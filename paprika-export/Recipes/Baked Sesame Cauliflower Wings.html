Baked Sesame Cauliflower Wings

★★★★★

Servings: 4 servings Source: peta.org

Ingredients

1 cup water or soy milk

1 cup flour (any kind will work—even gluten-free)

2 tsp. garlic powder

1 head cauliflower, chopped into pieces

1 Tbsp. sesame oil

1 tsp. sugar

1/2 cup soy sauce

1/4 cup rice vinegar

1 Tbsp. Sriracha

Sesame seeds, for garnish

Directions

Preheat the oven to 450°F.

Combine the water or soy milk, flour, and garlic powder in a bowl and stir until well combined.

Coat the cauliflower pieces with the flour mixture and place in a shallow baking dish. Bake for 18 minutes.

While the cauliflower is baking, combine the sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and Sriracha in a small bowl.

Pour the sauce mixture over the baked cauliflower and bake for an additional 5 to 8 minutes.

Garnish with sesame seeds and enjoy.
